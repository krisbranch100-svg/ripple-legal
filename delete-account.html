Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Ripple Time Map account and all associated data directly from within the app.

How to delete your account in the app:

  1. Open the Ripple Time Map app
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm when prompted
This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

  • Your account credentials (email and password)
  • Your profile information (display name)
  • Your subscription status and history

What is retained

  • Historical photos you contributed — these become part of the public archive and are retained to preserve the historical record. If you wish to have your contributed photos removed, contact us at rippletimemap@gmail.com

Can't access the app?

If you're unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:
rippletimemap@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

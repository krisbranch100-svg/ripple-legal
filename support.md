Frequently Asked Questions
How do I upload a photo?
Tap the Upload tab, choose a photo from your camera roll, add a title and approximate date, then pin it to its location on the map.
What kinds of photos can I upload?
Historical photos only — ideally pre-1990. Photos are reviewed by our AI and moderation team before appearing on the map.
My photo was rejected — why?
Photos may be rejected if they appear to be modern, are not of a real place, or contain inappropriate content. If you think your photo was rejected in error, email us.
How do I delete a photo I uploaded?
Email us at mrbubbaroo@gmail.com with the photo details and we'll remove it promptly.
How do I delete my account?
Email us at mrbubbaroo@gmail.com and we'll delete your account and all associated data.
The app isn't working — what should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem continues, email us with a description of what's happening and what device you're using.

Ripple is built and maintained by an independent developer. We appreciate your patience and feedback.
